The importance of undestanding multiple programming languages.

Understanding more than one programming language is very important. At first, when you feel attached to one language you may feel like you only need the one. However, that is the wrong assumption about learning multiple languages. Each language is unique and does something better than the other. As you learn more languages and figure out what they specilize in, you start to understand the power of knowing multiple languages for certain task. Lastly, overtime you may feel pressured to learn other languages. However, it is easier than you think, once you cement yourself in one or two languages, you are about to learn new langauges very quickly So don't feel discouraged about learning new languages, make fun of it and learn their strengths.
